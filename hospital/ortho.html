Orthopedic Care

Patients at Paediatric Intensive Care Unit are cared for by a multi-disciplinary team of doctors, specialist nurses, technicians, pharmacists, physiotherapists, dieticians, healthcare assistants and administrative support assistants. We have close links to many other services including psychologists and child care specialists. We are committed to providing family-centred care and encourage families to be an integral part of the team and present as much as they wish throughout their child's stay.
